More About Me

As a data-driven generalist, I thrive on transforming complex datasets into actionable insights. My portfolio showcases a versatile skillset, blending programming proficiency with a robust understanding of data analysis principles. My foundation in Mechanical Engineering from the University of the Philippines Diliman instilled a strong analytical mindset, a meticulous approach to problem-solving, and a deep appreciation for data's power to tell a story. This engineering background provided a rigorous framework for approaching challenges, which I now apply to diverse data-related projects.

My passion for data extends beyond technical proficiency. I believe in the power of clear communication and effective visualization to translate complex findings into easily digestible narratives. Whether developing machine learning models to predict SpaceX Falcon 9 landings or building interactive dashboards to analyze financial trends, I am driven by a desire to uncover hidden patterns and communicate data stories effectively. My projects demonstrate my ability to work with real-world data, from cleaning and preprocessing to building predictive models and creating impactful visualizations. Explore my portfolio to see how I translate raw data into impactful solutions, contributing to data-driven decision-making and positive outcomes. I am constantly seeking new opportunities to learn and grow in the ever-evolving field of data science, eager to tackle new challenges and contribute to innovative projects.
